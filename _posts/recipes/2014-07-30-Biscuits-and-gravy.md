## Ingredients
- 1 lb sausage, casing removed
- &frac13; cup flour
- 3 to four cups milk



## Instructions
1. Acquire [biscuits](../biscuits)
2. Fry sausage loose.
3. Sprinkle in flour and let the sausage absorb it
4. Add milk and season.
5. Allow to thicken and serve.
